How are issues tracked?

The issue tracker moves questions through the workflow of the relevant standard body to resolution.

  • Forum moderators and community members will submit unanswered questions for review. Moderators ensure they reach the appropriate standards body, where subject matter experts (SMEs) will be assigned to resolve the issues.
  • SMEs from the relevant standards bodies will develop authoritative answers.
  • Moderators will post authoritative answers back to the forum and knowledge base, where other members of the standards community with similar questions can easily find them.
